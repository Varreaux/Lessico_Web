Privacy policy — last updated July 18, 2026

NO DATA COLLECTION

Lessico does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The game runs fully offline: there are no accounts, no ads, no analytics, no trackers, and no third-party services.

Your progress (completed levels, reviewed words, audio settings, and language choices) is saved only on your device. Deleting the app deletes this data.
